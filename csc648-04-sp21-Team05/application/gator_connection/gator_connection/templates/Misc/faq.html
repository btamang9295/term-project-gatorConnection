Frequently Asked Questions - FAQ

One stop for all your questions!


1: Can I use Gator Connection if I'm not an SFSU student?

-Yes, but you won't have full access to our site. If you are a registered SFSU student then you are able to post in housing, items, and restaurants and more!


2: Can I post announcements as a regular user?

-No, you must have a registered Admin account to post. Depending on what admin account you register as you can post to the corresponding section. (For example: Athletics admin can only post to Athletics Announcements)


3: Can I request a listing if I am not a registered user?

-Sorry, you must be a registered SFSU user to login and request housing


4: What if I don't see a restaurant I like?

-No worries! You can request a restaurant if you are a registered user just make sure to have the necessary information such as hours, location, and etc


5: How do I know if I'm a registered?

-You will receive an email from Gator Connection to verify your registration as an SFSU student.


6: What is the map feature for?

-Our map feature is tool for you to see what is around our campus!


7: What type of items can I sell on Gator Connection?

-You can sell books, laptops, and school supplies.


8: What can I look for with the search bar?

-Search through our listings whether it is in Housing, Items, or Restaurants categories (For example: Under the Housing category search for "Parkmerced" and any housing listing that has been posted to Gator Connection will appear).


9: I forgot my password how can I access my account?

- Please select the "Forgot Password" option while logging in or fill out a contact us form located on our Home footer (bottom of the home page).


10: If I'm not a registered user can I use the Contact Us form?

- Yes, anyone can submit a contact us form! No need to have an SFSU email we appreciate your feedback.





If we missed a question, let us know! Please fill out a Contact Us form with any question you may have.
